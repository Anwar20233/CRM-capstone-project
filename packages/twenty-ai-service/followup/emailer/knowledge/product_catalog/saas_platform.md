# Product Catalog — SaaS Platform

## Overview
Cloud-based CRM and workflow automation platform for mid-market B2B teams.

## Key capabilities
- Pipeline management with stage-based automation
- Email and calendar integration
- AI-assisted follow-up recommendations
- Role-based access and audit logging

## Typical outcomes
- 20–30% faster follow-up cycles
- Improved forecast accuracy
- Centralized customer communication history

## Implementation
Standard rollout: discovery (1 week), configuration (2 weeks), training (1 week).
