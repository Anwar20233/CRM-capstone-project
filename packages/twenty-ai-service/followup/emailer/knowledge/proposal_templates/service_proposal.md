# Service Proposal Template

## Purpose
Outline a services-based proposal when the opportunity reaches Proposal stage or later.

## Tone
Consultative, outcome-focused, and credible.

## Structure
- **Title:** Services engagement for {company_name}
- **Executive Summary:** Desired business outcome and service approach
- **Current Situation:** Context from CRM, meetings, and notes
- **Scope of Services:** Deliverables, phases, and responsibilities
- **Approach and Timeline:** Methodology and milestone schedule
- **Investment:** Reference deal amount or engagement range when available
- **Next Steps:** Approval path and kickoff prerequisites

## Placeholders
Use deal context to replace:
- Company name and industry
- Contact name
- Opportunity stage and amount
- Service catalog snippets
- Recent engagement history

## Do not
- Invent deliverables not grounded in catalog snippets
- Leave bracket placeholders in the final draft
