# Re-Engagement Email Template

## Purpose
Re-open dialogue when deal risk is high and engagement has stalled.

## Tone
Empathetic, low-pressure, and value-focused. Avoid sounding desperate.

## Structure
- **Subject:** Check-in referencing prior conversation or company goal
- **Opening:** Acknowledge time passed since last touchpoint
- **Value reminder:** Briefly restate relevant benefit for their situation
- **Risk-aware ask:** One simple question or low-friction offer (e.g. 15-min call)
- **Closing:** Easy opt-out and professional sign-off

## Placeholders
Use deal context to replace:
- Contact name
- Company name
- Last known stage or activity
- Specific value proposition for their industry

## Do not
- Blame the prospect for going quiet
- Use aggressive urgency language
- Leave placeholder brackets in the final draft
