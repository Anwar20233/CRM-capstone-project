# Meeting Recap Email Template

## Purpose
Summarize a completed meeting and confirm agreed next steps.

## Tone
Appreciative, clear, and collaborative.

## Structure
- **Subject:** Meeting recap with company name
- **Opening:** Thank attendees for their time
- **Recap:** Bullet key discussion points from the meeting summary
- **Decisions:** List decisions or commitments made
- **Next steps:** Numbered action items with owners and dates
- **Closing:** Invite corrections and propose follow-up timing

## Sign-off
End every draft with this exact closing block (keep the rep name placeholder unless a sender name is in context):
```
Best regards,
[Your Name]
BeamData
```

## Placeholders
Use deal context to replace:
- Contact name
- Company name
- Meeting title and summary
- Attendee names
- Agreed next steps

## Do not
- Invent discussion points not supported by the meeting summary
- Leave bracket placeholders except `[Your Name]` or `[INSERT NAME]` in the sign-off
- Omit the BeamData sign-off line
